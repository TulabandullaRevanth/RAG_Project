Time Off and Leave Policy Guidelines for All Employees

Employees are entitled to 20 days paid time off per year, plus 5 additional personal days for flexible use. Leave accrues monthly at 1.67 days per month. Unused leave can be carried forward to next calendar year with maximum carryover of 5 days. Excess days over carryover limit are automatically forfeited with no compensation. Employees are entitled to 10 days paid sick leave annually for personal illness or medical appointments. Medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Unused sick days do not carryover to next year and are forfeited.

Female employees are entitled to 4 months paid maternity leave plus 2 months unpaid leave with job security guarantee. Parental responsibilities leave of 5 days per year is available to all parents regardless of gender. Leave request process requires submission to direct manager at least 2 weeks in advance except in emergency situations. Approval is based on business needs and department capacity. Requests submitted within 2 weeks require VP level approval. During peak seasons including Q4, leave may be restricted or require additional approval levels.

Sabbatical leave is available to employees with 5 or more years of service. Employees may request unpaid sabbatical leave of 1 to 3 months duration. Sabbatical must be approved by department head and HR director. Position is held during sabbatical with no benefits during the period. Bereavement leave of 5 days paid is provided for immediate family death. Extended family death provides 2 days paid leave. Flexible arrangements available if travel required for funeral services.
