Q4 2024 Budget Allocation and Financial Overview

The Q4 budget for fiscal year 2024 has been finalized and distributed across all departments. Finance department received $450,000 for operational expenses including software licenses, infrastructure maintenance, and personnel costs. Engineering department allocated $600,000 for new development initiatives and cloud infrastructure scaling. Sales department received $350,000 for marketing campaigns, client entertainment, and sales tools.

Total company budget for Q4: $2,100,000. Capital expenditure requests must be approved by CFO before procurement. All purchase orders above $50,000 require board approval. Department heads must submit variance reports if spending deviates by more than 10% from planned allocations. Emergency discretionary fund of $150,000 available for unexpected operational needs, requires VP-level approval. Year-end closing expected December 28. All invoices must be received by December 20 for Q4 inclusion. Quarterly bonuses will be distributed based on company performance achieving 95% of revenue targets.
