Employee Benefits and Compensation Package Details

Company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage through Blue Shield insurance carrier. Coverage includes medical, dental, and vision services. Company covers 80% of premium costs while employee pays 20%. Plan year runs from January to December. Open enrollment occurs annually from October 15 to October 31.

Retirement benefits include 401k plan with company match structure. Company matches 50% of first 6% of employee contribution with maximum 3% company contribution. Vesting schedule follows 25% annually for 4 years reaching 100% vested after 4 years of service. Employees become eligible for 401k after 30 days of employment. Life insurance is provided at no cost to employee with coverage equal to 2 times annual salary. Spouse and dependent coverage available at employee expense using group rates offering 30% below market rates.

Disability insurance includes short-term disability covering 60% of salary up to 12 weeks. Long-term disability covers 50% of salary after 12 weeks until age 65. Coverage obtained through group plan with managed benefits. Professional development budget of $2,500 per employee annually for training and development. Unused budget does not carry over to next year. Pre-approval required for expenses over $500.

Wellness program includes free gym membership up to $100 monthly, on-site health screenings annually, and mental health support through EAP Employee Assistance Program. Free counseling limited to 3 sessions per year per issue type. Paid holidays include 10 fixed holidays: New Year, MLK Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving plus 1 day, and Christmas. 2 additional floating holidays available per employee choice annually.
