Company Expense and Reimbursement Policy Guidelines

All business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence with supporting documentation. Meal reimbursement limits are $60 per day for solo travel and $75 per day when traveling with clients. Alcoholic beverages are not reimbursable under any circumstances. Receipts are required for all expenses exceeding $25.

Hotel accommodations during business travel follow tier system: standard hotels up to $150 per night in major cities, $120 in smaller cities, luxury hotels require VP approval. Mileage reimbursement follows federal rate of $0.655 per mile for 2024. Air travel policy mandates economy class for flights under 6 hours, business class requires CFO approval. Premium economy permitted for flights exceeding 8 hours. Seat upgrades at personal expense are not reimbursable. Ground transportation including taxi and Uber capped at $50 per trip.

Home office equipment allowance of $500 one-time per remote employee covers monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Personal computers are not covered. Internet stipend of $50 monthly provided for remote workers. Phone allowance of $75 monthly available for client-facing roles. Conference and training budget capped at $2,500 annually per employee with certification exams covered 100%. Training materials and books budgeted at $300 annually per employee.
